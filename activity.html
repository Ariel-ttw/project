Abundant activities located in the Arctic Circle.

Shaped by a diverse culture, it combines nature and humanities. You are invited to experience it together.

    • Thrilling outdoor skiing.

    Due to natural geographical conditions, the ski slopes here are natural snow rather than artificial snow. It covers a large area and is divided into a variety of slopes, suitable for all stages of the tourist experience. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced ski master, you can find a place for you in this snowy area.

    • Observe the aurora at night.

    In the Arctic Circle, there are natural advantages for astronomy enthusiasts to visit the Northern Lights. You can choose to enjoy it in the open-air glass room, or you can set up a tent in the wild with your guide to watch the gorgeous Northern Lights.

    • Chasing the footprints of polar bears

    Suitable for families to experience together is to observe wild polar bears and pass on the children's awareness of ecological environment protection.As the climate warms, glaciers melt. The living conditions of polar bears are increasingly threatened, and even on the verge of extinction. In this polar bear's natural habitat, tourists can see rare polar bears. When people take a boat to find their traces, they will gradually realize the importance of protecting polar bears.

Breathing fresh air quietly, waiting for the fish to hook, will make the experiencer feel relaxed, which can effectively relieve the pressure accumulated in the urban work.
